## Intro
Goal of this page is to document the purpose of the product.
Our success is in not developing a new product, it's in finding the right market and the right approach of delivering it.

## Features
### Multilingual menu
The main focus in the European market is delivering restaurant menus in multiple languages. And the static strings that make up the interfaces in a localized way. Take into account the differences in the number and date formats.

### Smart dining
Real time service calls. Order in restaurant without interacting with waiters. Have the credit card saved in app and pay conveniently.
